§ 03 — THE SYSTEM
Three pillars. Every role.

Three things every role gets — without adding another initiative.

NorthStar isn’t a tool with many features. It’s a role-based operating system organized around three repeating pillars that show up on every dashboard.

A
DO THE WORK

Workspace

A role-based dashboard with the tools and workflows each job actually needs each day — connected, not siloed.

  • Tools grouped by role responsibility
  • Embedded workflows that build on each other
  • One workspace replaces a stack of disconnected systems
B
GROW THE WORK

Pathways

Personalized learning anchored in real needs. Students get learning pathways. Adults get micro-credentials with authentic evidence.

  • Driven by student needs and role priorities
  • Evidence from real practice — not seat time
  • Continuous improvement, not one-and-done PD
C
GUIDE THE WORK

Coach

A virtual systems coach that prompts next-best-actions, reinforces routines, and translates learning into daily practice.

  • Just-in-time guidance, not another evaluator
  • Supports consistent routines without micromanagement
  • Helps every role focus on what matters most right now

§ 05 — MICRO-CREDENTIALS
From student need to system growth.

Focused professional learning, driven by student needs.

NorthStar micro-credentials are job-embedded, role-specific, and grounded in authentic evidence. They build individual practice and system-wide capacity at the same time.

Student needs drive professional learning. Professional learning strengthens adult practice. Adult practice grows the system.

FOR TEACHERS

Using Student Goal-Setting to Increase Ownership

Student need: low engagement or unclear learning targets.
Focus: goal-setting routines, feedback, reflection.
Evidence · student goals · progress reflections · instructional adjustments
FOR INSTRUCTIONAL COACHES

Designing Effective Coaching Cycles

Need: inconsistent coaching impact across classrooms.
Focus: goal-aligned cycles, feedback, evidence use.
Evidence · coaching plans · observation notes · teacher reflections
FOR SCHOOL LEADERSHIP

Leading a School-Wide Improvement Routine

Need: uneven implementation across classrooms.

Scaling Coaching Practices Across Schools

SYSTEM INTELLIGENCE

Meet Joey — the engine that turns evidence into focus.

Joey is NorthStar’s learning and assessment intelligence. He aggregates classroom and school evidence across the district, surfaces patterns of growth and gap, and connects student outcomes to instructional practices and adult learning — so leaders can target wrap-around support where it’s needed most.

Joey reveals the needs.
The system guides the response.

Paired with the NorthStar Systems Coach and the interactive Continuous Improvement Plan, Joey transforms day-to-day classroom activity into district-level clarity — without adding another report to chase.

★ INTERACTIVE CIP

Continuous improvement — living, not laminated.

“Every building team has its own interactive plan. The district plan analyzes them all.”

Goals are guided by Joey’s analysis. Meetings are structured by the Systems Coach. Progress is tracked in real time. Wrap-around coaching is targeted where student growth needs are greatest.

  • Goal recommendations from real evidence
  • Structured leadership agendas
  • Building & district plans aligned
  • Real-time progress monitoring
  • Wrap-around coaching targeting
  • Self-regulation & instructional needs
