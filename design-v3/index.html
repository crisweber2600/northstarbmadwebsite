PreK–8 Curriculum

A coherent learning journey.

Eight units of study per grade, literacy integrated into non-fiction science and social studies, vertically aligned across the entire PreK–8 experience.

  • 8units / grade
  • PreK–8vertical alignment

Learning Evidence

Evidence that grows with students.

Every student has a standards-based report card and a digital learning portfolio that builds across PreK–8.

Daily learning artifacts become long-term evidence of growth.

Professional Learning

Real classrooms, not theory.

Thousands of classroom videos let educators observe instruction and learning in real classrooms — no substitutes, no time away.

Job-embedded · Grounded in real practice · Connected to instruction.

Reading Access

Books at every level.

Thousands of books and learning resources written across reading levels — on the same learning topics — so students stay together while growing fast.

Science of Reading

The Written Code Continuum.

A systematic, sequential PreK–8 written code curriculum, grounded in the science of reading.

1Phonological & phonemic awareness
2Phonics & sound–symbol relationships
3Orthography & spelling patterns
4Morphology — prefixes, suffixes, Greek & Latin roots
5Grammar, syntax, sentence structure
6Academic vocabulary & word meaning
